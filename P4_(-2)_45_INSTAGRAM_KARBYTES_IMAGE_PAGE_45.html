While I am fundamentally a nihilist who believes that existence, and therefore life, is fundamentally devoid of purpose and of value, I do agree with the Instagram post depicted in the screenshot below that enjoying the passage of time is ultimately what makes being incarnated as a sentient being feel intrinsically worthwhile and that such a sense of worthwhileness pertains to all goal-oriented behavior and to all discovery-based experiences. To be succinct, I think that there is no “higher” purpose to human existence than merely having fun. The “purpose” of serving others in charitable ways is ultimately to help as many people as possible have as much fun as possible.
